# CareOrbit

Univeristy of Glasgow : WAD Team Project 25/26

CareOrbit is a digital healthcare management platform that helps patients manage their medical information in one place. The web app allows patients to securely log in, book virtual or in-person appointments, check and refill their medications, and access test results. 
The system will also allow users to view and download their documents, receive health-related notifications regarding appointments and vaccinations, and manage dependents within their account. 
Health providers will upload after-visit summaries and general notes. They should update medications according to the prescription and upload test results to patients.
The primary objective of this application is to enable effortless communication between patients and medical providers while delivering excellent services that increase the accessibility and organization of medical records.
